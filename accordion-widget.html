Account Questions

When logging in, click "Forgot your password?" You should receive a password reset email withing a few minutes with instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, we cannot view or retrieve your previous password.

If you do not receive the reset email, check your spam or junk folder and verify that you are using the correct email associated with your account.

If you still need assistance, contact your district representative or email support@aealearningonline.org.



You should reach out to your district contact first. If that is not possible, you can submit a support ticket by going here or by emailing support@aealearningonline.org.

Provide as much detail as possible (i.e. First Name, Last Name, previous Last Name if you have had a name change, BoEE folder number if known, prior emails if known). The more information you provide, the quicker we are able to support.

If you have worked in a public or private school district in Iowa, you will most likely already have an account.
